Realtime Sass Transpiler

Welcome to the realtime sass transpiler!

Welcome to the RST Project! This is a transpiler for Sass written completely in JavaScript. This transpiler have full support for the SCSS syntax of Sass.

Now you might be wondering; "But why would I ever want to transpile my Sass real-time?" Well, good question… Think about it this way: Now you don't need to download any extra software, pre-processors or such! Just link to the framework and it just works! Click me :D
